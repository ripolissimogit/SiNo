Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

SiNo ("the App") is a simple decision-making app. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any information from you. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no cookies.

Data Storage

The App stores your preferences (such as your selected preset) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page.
